# Tavern Zone - Parallax Layers

**Zone:** The Rusty Compile (Tavern)
**Purpose:** Rest, celebration, idle time, quest completion
**Mood:** Celebratory, cozy, relaxed
**Phases:** idle, complete, celebration

---

## Layer Structure

### far_background/ (depth: 0.2)
**What to draw:** Tavern interior back wall, windows
- Wood-paneled walls
- Windows showing night outside
- Back of tavern
- Trophy/decoration mounts
**Placeholder color:** `#2a1a1a`

### mid_background/ (depth: 0.5)
**What to draw:** Bar counter, shelves with bottles
- Wooden bar counter
- Bottles and kegs on shelves
- Tables and chairs
- Fireplace (side wall)
**Placeholder color:** `#3a2a2a`

### foreground/ (depth: 0.8)
**What to draw:** Tables, chairs, mugs, decorations
- Wooden tables in foreground
- Chairs around tables
- Mugs and plates
- Quest completion decorations
**Placeholder color:** `#4a3a2a`

### overlay/ (depth: 1.0)
**What to draw:** Fireplace glow, celebration effects
- Warm fireplace light
- Confetti (on quest complete)
- Sparks from fireplace
- Celebration particle effects
**Placeholder color:** `rgba(255, 140, 0, 0.3)`

---

## Key Elements to Include
- 🍺 Bar counter (ale serving)
- 🔥 Fireplace (warm ambiance)
- 🪑 Tables & chairs (party gathering spots)
- 🎉 Celebration decorations (on success)
- 🏆 Trophy/achievement displays

---

## Two Modes
1. **Idle Mode:** Quiet, a few patrons, calm
2. **Celebration Mode:** Active, decorations, confetti, victory

---

## Color Palette
- Warm woods (oak, mahogany)
- Fireplace orange/yellow glow
- Gold accents (celebration)
- Cozy, inviting atmosphere

---

## Reference Mood
- Fantasy RPG tavern
- Victory celebration space
- Warmth and camaraderie
- "We did it! Time to celebrate!"
